Honest Answers

The questions you're actually asking

"Is it worth the money?"

Membership pays for itself with one discounted event. Members save $5–$145 on every registration, plus exclusive access to PMP prep pricing, the job program, and mentoring. All included.

"I don't have time for another thing."

Most events are 1-hour virtual sessions you can attend during lunch. No commute. No pressure to come every month. Show up when it works for you.

"I don't have my PMP yet. Do I belong?"

Absolutely. Many members join to get their PMP, not because they already have it. No credential is required. Just curiosity and a willingness to grow.

"I won't know anyone."

That's exactly what orientation is for. It's a small-group session designed for people who are brand new. You'll meet the membership team and other newcomers. No one expects you to know anyone.
